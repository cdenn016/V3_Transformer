## Headline: theory verification of the pure path

The user's central question — is the gauge-VFE math implemented correctly on the theoretically
pure path — received a strong, numerically evidenced YES from four independent experts who ran
executable probes rather than reading docstrings:

- **Differential geometry (CLEAN, no findings).** Covariance transport is the congruence/sandwich
  `Omega Sigma Omega^T` everywhere, with no similarity-transport mixing anywhere
  (`transport.py` full branch, oracle and kernel both route through it). The affine-invariant SPD
  retraction matches the canonical `Sigma^{1/2} expm(Sigma^{-1/2} dSigma Sigma^{-1/2}) Sigma^{1/2}`
  to rel. err 5e-7, satisfies R(S,0)=S and dR(0)=id, and the diagonal arm coincides with the full
  arm on diagonal input. The flat-transport cocycle holds numerically: `Omega_ii = I` (2.4e-7),
  `Omega_ij Omega_jk = Omega_ik` (3.6e-7), `Omega_ij Omega_ji = I` (3.6e-7). `_EighDamped`
  backward matches stock `eigh` to 1e-12+. Killing/pullback preconditioners are SPD and the
  natural-gradient sidedness is correct.
- **Gauge theory (pure path CLEAN).** Generator algebras verified: all 21 sp(2m,R) generators
  satisfy `J A + A^T J = 0` at full rank, so(N) skew, `tied_block_glk = kron(I_n, E)`. Global
  gauge equivariance of the pure flat path: in-group energy residual 4.6e-5 / beta residual
  2.9e-6, against out-of-group controls 0.92 / 0.51. Holonomy metrics certify flatness (3.2e-6).
  The head mixer is exactly equivariant under tied gauge (1.6e-7) and breaks under untied
  block_glk exactly as CLAUDE.md exception #2 documents. The two gauge findings are RoPE-specific
  (below), not pure-path defects.
- **Information geometry (one low finding).** Diagonal and full Gaussian KL closed forms match
  `torch.distributions.kl_divergence` to ~1e-6; the Renyi closed form matches scipy quadrature at
  alpha = 0.3/0.5/0.8 in the exact q||p direction; Renyi → KL as alpha → 1; the generic
  Bregman/log-partition path agrees with the closed forms for both families (dual coordinates
  consistent, including the full-Gaussian matrix statistic). KL directions all match the
  manuscript: self-coupling KL(q_i||p_i), coupling KL(q_i||Omega q_j), hyper-prior KL(s||h),
  decode KL(belief||vocab-prior). The Fisher natural gradient is exactly right:
  `nat_mu = Sigma grad_mu`, `nat_sigma = 2 Sigma grad_sigma Sigma`, both verified to 0 error.
- **Variational structure (two findings, both toggle-gated).** `free_energy()` matches the
  canonical F term-by-term, including the attention-entropy term `tau beta log(beta/pi)` with
  `pi = softmax(log_prior)`. Softmax beta IS the stationary point of the F the code evaluates:
  max projected gradient ~8e-8 at `beta = softmax(B - E/tau)` versus 0.30 at the wrong
  stationarity candidate. The analytic filtering kernel's gradient equals autograd-of-F to ~5e-7
  (mu and sigma), so the E-step descends the F it evaluates. Canonical vs entropy-suppressed
  surrogate is not silently mixed within a channel (`include_attention_entropy=False` correctly
  re-routes to the autograd oracle of the surrogate). EM separation is clean: the E-step sees no
  targets; the M-step moves only PriorBank tables plus sanctioned exception parameters, with an
  exact-coverage optimizer guard. The observation likelihood is genuinely Sigma-dependent (the
  decode is KL(q||pi_v) with trace and log-sigma terms, not a point estimate at mu); the
  `log_likelihood` argument of `free_energy()` is a dead stub with no caller.
- **Pure-path existence (the user's standing constraint): VERIFIED.** A grep-sweep of `nn.`
  across `vfe3/` found zero undocumented NN compute: every `nn.Parameter` is inert belief/prior
  storage or one of the three sanctioned exceptions; decode routes through the KL-to-prior kernel
  whenever `use_prior_bank=True` (`prior_bank.py:257`); flat Regime-I transport, head mixer off,
  and canonical entropy-included F are all reachable, and every E-step-relevant config field is
  threaded `model -> vfe_stack -> vfe_block -> e_step -> e_step_iteration`.
