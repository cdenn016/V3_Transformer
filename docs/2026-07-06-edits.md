# 2026-07-06 edits

## Learnable kappa training-history figures (branch `feat/kappa-learnable-figures-20260706`)

Added automatic end-of-run history figures for learnable softmax temperatures. When `learnable_kappa_beta` or `learnable_kappa_gamma` creates a live `log_kappa_*` parameter, `train()` now logs `kappa_*_mean` and `kappa_*_var` to `metrics.csv` on each metrics row. The mean and population variance are computed across irrep blocks/heads from the live `exp(log_kappa_*)` values.

`finalize_run()` now routes those history columns through `_save_figures()` and writes `kappa_beta_history.png` and/or `kappa_gamma_history.png` automatically when the corresponding columns exist. The new `vfe3.viz.figures.plot_kappa_history()` helper plots kappa mean by training step and shades mean +/- sqrt(variance), clipped at zero because kappa is positive by construction.

Tests added/updated: `tests/test_learnable_kappa.py` checks the metrics columns are written during a tiny training run, and `tests/test_reporting_additions.py` checks the plot helper plus automatic history PNG emission. Verification: `python -m pytest tests/test_learnable_kappa.py tests/test_reporting_additions.py tests/test_run_artifacts.py --junitxml=C:\tmp\vfe3-kappa-figures-pytest.xml`; XML summary `tests=52 failures=0 errors=0 skipped=0`.

## Learnable kappa per-block + tau trajectory figure (branch `feat/kappa-per-block-tau`)

Extended the aggregate figures above with a per-irrep-block view. `train()` now also logs one `kappa_<ch>_b<i>` and one `tau_<ch>_b<i> = kappa_b * sqrt(d_b)` column per irrep block (for the beta and gamma channels), alongside the existing mean/var. New `vfe3.viz.figures.plot_kappa_block_trajectory()` draws a 2x2 grid (rows = the beta/gamma channels present, columns = kappa and the effective temperature tau), one line per irrep block; `_save_figures()` auto-emits `kappa_block_trajectory.png` when the per-block columns exist. History-only: it reads the values logged during training straight from the live `log_kappa_*`, so it does not pass through the viz extractors flagged in M1 of the 2026-07-06 audit.

Tests: `test_training_logs_per_block_kappa_and_tau` (per-block kappa/tau columns written, `tau_b == kappa_b*sqrt(d_b)`), `test_kappa_block_trajectory_renders_per_block_kappa_and_tau`, `test_save_figures_emits_kappa_block_trajectory`.
