# Memo — debate-expert-philosophy-of-science — red — opening — blocks-k48-gauge-block-scaling-axis

## Lens
Philosophy of science — falsifiability, scope, theory-ladenness, confirmation bias, progressive vs. degenerative research programmes, novel-construction-vs-rediscovery, manuscript-as-authority circularity.

## Frame check
The claim is a five-fold conjunction — "genuine AND publishable AND parameter-efficient AND a distinct scaling axis AND not artifact/confound" — and it fails as a well-formed empirical proposition at three of the five joints.

First, the conjunction smuggles an interpretation into an observation. The observation is a monotone, three-seed-robust CE decrease (PPL 124.6 to 92.2) as the GL block widens at fixed `embed_dim=48`. That observation is real. The interpretation — "a distinct parameter-efficient scaling axis complementary to width-scaling" — is a much stronger claim that the observation does not license. A single monotone trend curve is one manipulation, not an axis with a scaling law.

Second, "distinct scaling axis" is not a sharp, falsifiable claim, because the exponent that would characterize the axis is observer-dependent on identical CE data: 0.929 versus `n_params` (with a confidence interval of [0.07, 1.73]), 0.181 versus `n_gen`, and degenerate (R^2 = 0.17) versus analytic FLOPs (`01_evidence.md`, line 61). A quantity whose value swings by 5x depending on the analyst's free choice of x-axis, with a CI that spans from "essentially no scaling" (0.07) to "super-linear" (1.73), forbids almost no future observation. By Popper's own criterion that is a scientifically weak claim, not a strong one.

Third, the causal core — "gauge structure per se," which is what carries "genuine" and "parameter-efficient" — is confounded by construction. The design co-varies three knobs simultaneously as `g` grows: total `n_params` (3.62x), `n_heads` (16 to 2), and block width `g` (`01_evidence.md`, line 51). No discriminating control was trained: no plain `V x m` learned table of matched size, no matched-parameter dense head-mix (`01_evidence.md`, line 80). Attributing the gain to gauge structure while three manipulations move together is the textbook Duhem-Quine problem: the negative or positive result of a compound manipulation does not designate which component produced it.

Fourth, "complementary to width-scaling" rests on a cross-sweep comparison across an exact 2.0x token-budget gap (same `data_sha256`), which confounds parameter-scaling with data-scaling for any absolute-PPL or floor comparison (Hoffmann 2022 `L(N,D)`). "Complementary" is therefore asserted, not established on equal footing.

The honest residue after the frame check is the narrow sub-claim the compound clause itself names as its fallback (`00_claim.md`, line 23): at fixed `embed_dim=48`, enlarging the GL block lowers CE monotonically across GL3 to GL24 on a 245.76M-token budget, robust across three seeds. That sub-claim is well-formed, falsifiable, and locally corroborated. The compound claim is none of the three.

## Steelman of the opposing position
`blocks_K48` is a use-novel, previously unpublished direction whose monotone, three-seed-robust CE gain on half the token budget — with the per-token active working set held near-constant while total capacity grows — is exactly the sparse / conditional-computation efficiency signature, and so counts as a genuine progressive extension of the program along an axis the manuscript has never explored.

## My position (in service of red)
The compound claim is not well-formed and not honestly evidenced: its "distinct scaling axis" conjunct has no observer-independent exponent (Popper — a claim compatible with every x-axis choice forbids nothing), its "gauge structure per se / parameter-efficient" conjunct is a confounded three-knob manipulation with no discriminating control run (Duhem-Quine), and its "complementary to width-scaling" conjunct is confounded by an exact 2x data gap; only the narrow monotone-CE sub-claim survives, so the correct disposition is REMAND.

## Evidence
- **Popper, *Conjectures and Refutations* (1963), ch. 1.** "A theory which is not refutable by any conceivable event is non-scientific. Irrefutability is not a virtue of a theory (as people often think) but a vice." As restated in the Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy ("Karl Popper"): "If a theory is incompatible with possible empirical observations it is scientific; conversely, a theory which is compatible with all such observations ... is unscientific." Application: an exponent that takes any value in [0.07, 1.73] depending on the chosen x-axis is compatible with almost every observation; it fails to forbid, and so fails as the sharp content of a "scaling axis" claim.
- **Lakatos, *The Methodology of Scientific Research Programmes* (1978).** A problemshift is theoretically progressive only if "each new theory in the sequence must have excess empirical content over its predecessor; it must predict novel and hitherto unexpected facts," and empirically progressive only if "some of the new 'facts' that the theory predicts must turn out to be true" (as quoted, SEP "Imre Lakatos"). Application: the gauge-axis hypothesis has predicted and corroborated no novel fact that a rival raw-capacity hypothesis does not equally predict; a monotone loss curve is not a novel corroborated prediction that discriminates the two, so this is not (yet) a progressive problemshift.
- **Duhem, *The Aim and Structure of Physical Theory* (1906), p. 187** (via SEP "Underdetermination of Scientific Theory"). "The physicist can never subject an isolated hypothesis to experimental test, but only a whole group of hypotheses; when the experiment is in disagreement with his predictions, what he learns is that at least one of the hypotheses constituting this group is unacceptable and ought to be modified; but the experiment does not designate which one should be changed." Application: with `n_params`, `n_heads`, and `g` moving together, the CE improvement cannot be assigned to the gauge block rather than to raw capacity or head-count reduction. The experiment does not designate the cause.
- **Quine, "Two Dogmas of Empiricism" (1951), p. 42.** "The unit of empirical significance is the whole of science." Application: the CE improvement corroborates the whole entangled configuration (linear-decode ablation `use_prior_bank=False`, `use_head_mixer=True`, model channel, and gauge block together — `01_evidence.md`, lines 16-24), not the gauge block as an isolable unit.
- **Cartwright, *The Dappled World* (1999), p. 50.** A nomological machine is "a fixed (enough) arrangement of components, or factors, with stable (enough) capacities that in the right stable (enough) environment will, with repeated operation, give rise to the kind of regular behaviour that we represent in [L]." Exporting the regularity outside the machine that grounds it is inferential overreach. Application: the monotone CE law was produced inside one specific machine — 245.76M tokens, single layer, single E-step, that toggle stack. "Complementary to width-scaling" exports it to a different machine (491.52M tokens) where it was never run.
- **Worrall, "Prediction and accommodation revisited" (2014).** The "no double use" principle: a single body of evidence cannot both fix a theory's free content and then count as independent support for the theory so fixed. Application: the scaling exponent is read off the very CE data it is meant to characterize; it independently corroborates nothing, and its non-uniqueness across x-axes shows the "axis" is being accommodated, not predicted.
- **Hoffmann et al. 2022 (Chinchilla), source of truth.** `L(N,D) = E + A/N^alpha + B/D^beta`: loss depends jointly on parameters and data. Application: an exact 2.0x gap in D between the two sweeps is a first-order confound for any absolute-loss or floor comparison, so "complementary to width-scaling" is not established at equal D.

## Falsifiability assessment of the claim
The conjuncts have different falsifiability status, and separating them is itself the finding.

The empirical sub-claim (monotone CE decrease across GL3 to GL24 at 245.76M tokens, three seeds) is falsifiable and locally corroborated: a non-monotone or seed-fragile curve would refute it; the data do not.

The causal conjunct ("gauge structure per se," carrying "genuine" and "parameter-efficient") is falsifiable in principle by a specific experiment that was not run: train a non-gauge `V x n_gen` learned lookup table of matched size (and, separately, a matched-parameter dense head-mix) at the matched 491.52M-token budget. If either matches or beats GL24's PPL (92.15), the "gauge structure per se" attribution is refuted, because a structure-free capacity control reproduced the gain. Because that discriminating experiment has not been run (`01_evidence.md`, lines 79-80), the causal conjunct is currently untested, not corroborated — a live claim awaiting its refuting trial, not a supported one.

The "distinct scaling axis" conjunct is, as stated, under-falsifiable: it has no observer-independent exponent, so there is no single number a future run could contradict. To become falsifiable it must first name its x-axis and its predicted exponent in advance; only then can a run refute it. That it currently cannot is a finding, not a technicality.

## Confidence
HIGH — the confound (three co-varying knobs, no control), the exact-2x data gap, and the axis-dependent exponent are stipulated facts in `01_evidence.md`, not contested inferences. A single trained matched-size non-gauge control at the matched 491.52M-token budget that still underperformed GL24 would rescue the causal conjunct and shift me toward accepting a narrowed "structured-capacity is more efficient than unstructured-capacity" claim.
