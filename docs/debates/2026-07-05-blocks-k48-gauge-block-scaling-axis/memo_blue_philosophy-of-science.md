# Memo — debate-expert-philosophy-of-science — BLUE — round 1 — blocks-k48-gauge-block-scaling-axis

## Lens
Philosophy of science — falsifiability, scope, theory-ladenness, confirmation bias, progressive vs. degenerative research programmes, novel-construction-vs-rediscovery, manuscript-as-authority circularity.

## Verdict
Two conjuncts survive intact ("genuine" and "distinct new direction"); one survives only in a demoted phenomenological form ("not a metric artifact" — the CE drop is real, but the flat working set IS definitional); and two force a remand ("parameter-efficient" and "publishable as-is scaling axis/exponent"). The sub-claim BLUE should defend: *a robust, monotonic, seed-stable cross-entropy improvement (PPL 124.6 to 92.2) along a genuinely new, previously-unpublished fixed-`embed_dim` gauge-block-partition axis, whose per-token decode-bound working set is constant by construction — reportable as a complementary structural knob, not as a standalone parameter-efficiency scaling law.*

## Argument

**Separate the robust observation from the contested interpretation.** The observation is: hold `embed_dim=48`, vary the gauge partition GL3→GL24, and cross-entropy falls strictly monotonically, seed-stable (per-label PPL std ≤ 1.08, far below the ~4.5-PPL steps; independently recomputed). This is a real manipulated variable (block width g) producing a real, reproducible effect. The contested interpretation is a *parameter-efficiency scaling exponent*, which is a claim about a law fitted over an x-axis.

**Hacking / Duhem-Quine: the observation is metric-robust; the efficiency exponent is not.** Hacking's thesis is that experiment "has a life of its own, independent of theory," and that observations "remain robust despite changes in the theory of the apparatus or in the theory of the phenomenon" (Hacking, *Representing and Intervening*, 1983, ch. on the life of experiment). The loss-vs-g regularity passes this test: it is monotonic in g however you choose to plot the parameter axis. The RED "vertical column" objection is itself frame-laden — it depends on selecting `active_params/token` as the x-axis — so it cannot by itself demote the *effect*. What it does demote is the *efficiency reading*, which has no life of its own: it lives entirely inside one metric.

**Cartwright: a phenomenological regularity is real and reportable without a clean fundamental law.** Cartwright's central thesis is a "tradeoff between generality and truth: the fundamental laws achieve the former at the expense of the latter, while phenomenological laws obtain the latter at the expense of the former" (Cartwright, *How the Laws of Physics Lie*, 1983, Introduction). She is a realist about phenomenological laws and an instrumentalist about fundamental laws. Mapped here: "loss decreases monotonically with block size" is a phenomenological law BLUE can be a realist about; "PPL scales as n_params^α with a reportable α" is a fundamental-law dress that, on this evidence, would be — in Cartwright's terms — a lie purchased with generality.

**Lakatos: the observation is a progressive contribution; the efficiency framing risks degeneration.** For a problemshift to be progressive it "must have excess empirical content over its predecessor and predict novel and hitherto unexpected facts," and be empirically progressive in that "some of that novel content has to be corroborated" (Lakatos, *The Methodology of Scientific Research Programmes*, 1978). The block-partition sweep supplies exactly this excess content: a phenomenon (monotonic loss drop at fixed `embed_dim`) that is absent from and not derivable by the published width-axis work, and it is corroborated (seed-stable). That is a genuine progressive increment. The degenerating move would be to rescue an artifact by re-labeling the x-axis so the working set stays "flat" and the story reads as efficiency — accommodating a known fact by metric choice rather than predicting a new one.

**Popper: the claim is falsifiable, which is a virtue.** "A theory which is not refutable by any conceivable event is non-scientific. Irrefutability is not a virtue of a theory (as people often think) but a vice" (Popper, *Conjectures and Refutations*, 1963). This claim forbids specific outcomes (below), so it clears demarcation — unlike a "novel construction" with no canonical counterpart. BLUE should press this: the claim earns scientific standing precisely because it stakes testable predictions.

**Guard against BLUE's own circularity (my mandatory flag).** BLUE's most tempting support — "the per-token working set stays ~constant, so it's efficient" — is inadmissible. The evidence shows `active = 5·V·K + 2·K + n_gen`, whose only sweep-varying term is `+n_gen` (+1,008 total = 0.008%). The metric is *defined* by the project (`run_artifacts.py:597-603`, flagged in-pack as "docstring, not authority") to hold the working set flat. Using that flatness as evidence for efficiency is manuscript-as-authority circularity: the metric that certifies the claim was constructed to have the certifying property. BLUE must not lean on it; the honest efficiency question is settled by FLOPs and wall-time (below), not by this proxy.

## Concessions / remand
- **Weakest conjunct — "publishable scaling exponent" — fails.** The fitted exponent is axis-dependent on identical CE data (0.929 vs n_params, CI [0.07, 1.73]; 0.181 vs n_gen; degenerate R²=0.17 vs analytic-FLOPs) over a compressed 3.62× x-range. A power law with a sign-ambiguous CI and no invariant across axes is not a reportable exponent. Kaplan-style scaling laws earn credibility by spanning many orders of magnitude; 3.62× cannot.
- **"Parameter-efficient" fails as stated.** At matched total n_params, blocks_K48 is *less* efficient than grow_K_GL10 by +4.8 to +12.8 PPL; real transport FLOPs grow as g² (9→576, 64×) and wall-time is U-shaped (min at GL8), so the compute picture is not flat. This partially self-falsifies on existing evidence.
- **Half-token confound is real and unaddressed.** blocks_K48 ran on 245.76M tokens vs grow_K's 491.52M. Under Hoffmann `L(N,D)=E+A/N^α+B/D^β`, a 2× difference in D is a first-order confound for any cross-sweep absolute-loss or floor comparison; no matched-budget blocks run exists.
- **Causal attribution to "gauge structure per se" is unestablished.** No non-gauge matched-size control (a plain `V×m` table or matched-param dense head-mix) was trained; the design co-varies three knobs (n_params 3.62×, n_heads 16→2, block width g).

## Falsification conditions
1. **Matched-token-budget run.** Retrain blocks_K48 at 491.52M tokens. If the monotonic gain collapses into seed noise once D matches grow_K, the "genuine new axis" reading is refuted as a data-budget/optimization-transient artifact.
2. **Non-gauge matched-size control.** Train a plain `V×m` learned table (or matched-param dense head-mix) with `m = n_gen`, no gauge-block structure. If it matches/beats the GLg curve at matched added params, "gauge structure per se" is refuted — the effect is raw capacity.
3. **Efficiency-dominance test.** At matched total n_params (already available), blocks is +4.8 to +12.8 PPL worse — this already falsifies "parameter-efficient" as dominance over width.
4. **Metric-robustness test.** If the monotonic loss-vs-g regularity vanishes under any reasonable x-axis (it does not — it is monotonic in g itself), the phenomenological sub-claim would fall. It survives; the *exponent* does not.

## Newly-discovered canon (for 01b_extended_evidence.md)
- **Lakatos, *The Methodology of Scientific Research Programmes* (1978)**, progressive/degenerating criterion (see SEP "Imre Lakatos," https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/lakatos/): "theoretically progressive" requires excess empirical content predicting novel facts; "empirically progressive" requires some of that content be corroborated — the operational test for whether the block-axis is a real increment or a re-labeled artifact.
- **Cartwright, *How the Laws of Physics Lie* (1983)**, Introduction (SEP summary; PhilPapers CARNGN): the generality-vs-truth tradeoff and realism about phenomenological (not fundamental) laws — licenses "reportable regularity" while denying "publishable exponent."
- **Hacking, *Representing and Intervening* (1983)**, "The life of experiment" (SEP "Experiment in Physics," §3.1, https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/physics-experiment/): experiment "has a life of its own, independent of theory"; robust observation vs. theory-laden metric — separates the CE effect from the efficiency proxy.
- **Popper, *Conjectures and Refutations* (1963)**: "Irrefutability is not a virtue of a theory but a vice" — the demarcation warrant for treating the falsifiers above as the claim's scientific credential.
- **Hoffmann et al., *Training Compute-Optimal LLMs* (Chinchilla, 2022)**: `L(N,D)=E+A/N^α+B/D^β`, compute-optimal ≈20 tokens/param — the D-confound formalism for the half-token gap.

## Confidence
HIGH — that the empirical regularity and new-direction conjuncts survive while "publishable exponent" and "parameter-efficient" force a remand. A matched-token-budget blocks run plus a non-gauge matched-size control would shift me: if both held, the narrower sub-claim would strengthen toward the full claim; if either failed, even the phenomenological reading narrows further.
