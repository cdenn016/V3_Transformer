## B3 / #14 — Fisher nat-grad vs raw-Euclidean E-step mean arm

- `vfe3/config.py`: new `e_step_mu_precond: 'fisher'|'raw'` (default `fisher`, validated). `fisher`
  descends the Fisher natural gradient `nat_mu = Σ·grad_mu`; `raw` descends the raw `grad_mu`. The SPD
  σ retraction is unchanged either way, isolating the **mean** arm (the σ-sector already whitens by
  1/σ in the affine retraction, so it's correctly out of scope).
- `vfe3/inference/e_step.py`: `e_step_iteration` selects the mean step on the toggle (byte-identical
  `fisher` path); `free_energy_value` accepts-and-ignores it (so the `e_step` `_f_diag` `**kwargs`
  forward doesn't TypeError). Threaded via `vfe3/model/block.py` (belief E-step) + `extract._iter_kwargs`;
  the s-channel E-step stays `fisher`.
- `ablation.py`: `fisher_mu_precond` sweep (fisher/raw × n_e_steps{1,3,5}, `requires e_phi_lr=0`) +
  `_plot_mu_precond` driver. `vfe3/viz/figures.py`: `mu_precond` figure (PPL vs n_e_steps, two lines).
